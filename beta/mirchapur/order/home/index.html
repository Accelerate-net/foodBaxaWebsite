About Us

Mirchapur is a perfect place in town to taste the best Arabic Food and BBQ, established as one of the most popular entrant into the string which serves Middle-East food along with Indian and Chinese. Authentic Arabian and Indian cuisines made with passion and attention to detail treat your taste buds well.

The first outlet of 'Mirchapur' was opened on 11th October 2009 at Adyar, in the heart of Chennai, with a vision of providing best quality food and authentic cuisine. The primary challenges of being a multi-cuisine restaurant are to maintain the authenticity, food innovation, hospitality and to incorporate new ideas in restaurant business in every aspect. Very keen in maintaining the food quality and taste, we also desire Mirchapur to be preferred for its refreshing ambience to dine in even for an impromptu lunch or dinner.

With a high degree of commitment and quality, our restaurant chain has expanded gloriously with 6 branches in Chennai, 2 in Bangalore, 1 in Madurai and we are opening soon in Qatar. Without compromising on quality and service, we take every step to make your dining experience, a pleasurable one.
